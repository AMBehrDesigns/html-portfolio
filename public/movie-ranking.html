Masha's Top 3

A list of movies that Masha has loved this year

Come and See

directed by Elem Klimov

This is a harrowing anti-war film set in Belarus during the later part of WWII. It follows a young boy who is enlisted in the Belarussian partisans and the horrors he faces there.

Drive my Car

directed by Ryusuke Hamaguchi

After the death of his wife, a stage director must reconnect with his humanity as he directs one of Chekhov's darkest comedies. His driver, a woman who looks like his late wife, also shares a similar grief.

Moonlight

directed by Barry Jenkins

A young black boy comes to terms with his reality as a gay person of color in the American south.
